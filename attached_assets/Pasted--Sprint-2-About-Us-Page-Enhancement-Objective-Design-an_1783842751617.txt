# Sprint 2 – About Us Page Enhancement

## Objective

Design and develop a premium "About Us" page for the official website of **Right Ewaste Recycle Private Limited**.

This page should not feel like a generic company profile. Instead, it should tell a compelling story about the company's purpose, mission, and commitment to responsible e-waste recycling.

The overall design should match the premium look and feel of the existing website while maintaining consistency in typography, colors, spacing, animations, and responsiveness.

**Important:** Do not redesign the website or change the existing project architecture. Only enhance the About page while preserving reusable components, routing, responsiveness, and the current design language.

Use professional placeholder content and high-quality dummy images where actual company information is not yet available.

---

# Overall Design Style

The About page should feel:

* Professional
* Premium
* Modern
* Trustworthy
* Environmentally Responsible
* Clean
* Spacious
* Corporate

Use subtle Framer Motion animations throughout the page.

Avoid clutter.

Maintain generous whitespace.

# Future Ready

All placeholder content, statistics, images, and milestones should be stored in easily editable objects or configuration files so they can later be replaced with actual company information without modifying the layout.

# Final Goal

The About Us page should make visitors feel that Right Ewaste Recycle Private Limited is a trustworthy, modern, environmentally responsible organization dedicated to solving the growing challenge of electronic waste. The page should balance storytelling, education, and professionalism while maintaining a premium corporate appearance consistent with the rest of the website.
