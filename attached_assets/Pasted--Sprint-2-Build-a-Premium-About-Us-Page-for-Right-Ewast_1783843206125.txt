# Sprint 2 - Build a Premium About Us Page for Right Ewaste Recycle Pvt. Ltd.

## Objective

Design and develop a completely new **About Us** page for the official website of **Right Ewaste Recycle Private Limited**.

The About page should be inspired by the storytelling style and premium corporate experience found on leading environmental and recycling company websites (such as RecycleKaro), but it **must not copy their design, layout, content, branding, or assets**.

Instead, create a unique, modern, premium experience that tells the story of Right Ewaste Recycle Private Limited while maintaining the existing design language of this project.

This page should feel like it was designed by a premium digital agency.

Maintain the existing project architecture, reusable components, animations, responsiveness, typography, colors, and routing.

---

# Overall Design Philosophy

The About page should tell a story.

Instead of simply displaying company information, the visitor should gradually understand:

• Why the company exists

• The environmental problem

• Our mission

• Our approach

• Our commitment

• Our impact

• Why customers should trust us

The scrolling experience should feel smooth and engaging.

Every section should naturally lead into the next.

---

# Code Quality

Use reusable components.

Store all placeholder text, statistics, values, images and timeline items in configuration objects instead of hardcoding them.

Follow the existing architecture.

Do not modify unrelated pages.

---

# Final Goal

The About Us page should feel like a premium corporate presentation rather than a standard company profile. Visitors should finish the page understanding the company's purpose, values, expertise, and environmental commitment, while feeling confident that Right Ewaste Recycle Private Limited is a trustworthy and modern e-waste recycling partner. The experience should be immersive, visually rich, responsive, and consistent with the rest of the website.
